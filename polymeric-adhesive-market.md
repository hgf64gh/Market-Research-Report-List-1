<p><strong>The Polymeric Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Adhesive</li><li>Thermoplastic Adhesive</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric adhesive market encompasses various types, primarily thermosetting and thermoplastic adhesives, alongside others like reactive and pressure-sensitive adhesives. Thermosetting adhesives cure through a chemical reaction, providing strong, durable bonds suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives remain pliable when heated and can be remolded, making them versatile for various applications. Other types include reactive adhesives that bond upon curing with a chemical, and pressure-sensitive adhesives that adhere with minimal pressure, catering to diverse industries and needs.</p></p>
