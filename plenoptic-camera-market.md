<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plenoptic camera market is primarily segmented into two types: standard plenoptic cameras and focused plenoptic cameras. Standard plenoptic cameras capture images while simultaneously recording depth information, allowing for post-capture refocusing and three-dimensional rendering. In contrast, focused plenoptic cameras are designed to optimize image sharpness and detail at specific focal points, making them suitable for applications requiring high precision, such as scientific imaging and industrial inspection. Both types enhance image versatility and provide unique advantages for various imaging needs.</p></p>
