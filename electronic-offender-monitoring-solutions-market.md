<p><strong>The Electronic Offender Monitoring Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radio Frequency (RF)</li><li>Global Positioning System (GPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Offender Monitoring Solutions market primarily includes two types: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS). RF monitoring involves using radio signals to track an offender’s location within a defined perimeter, often through ankle bracelets, and is commonly used for home confinement. In contrast, GPS monitoring provides real-time location tracking via satellite technology, allowing authorities to monitor offenders’ movements beyond set boundaries. Both technologies enhance public safety and facilitate better management of offenders in the community.</p></p>
