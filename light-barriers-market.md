<p><strong>The Light Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNP output</li><li>NPN output</li><li>OSE output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light barriers, or photoelectric sensors, are categorized by their output types, which determine their application compatibility. PNP output sensors switch on when they detect light and provide a positive voltage signal to the load. NPN output sensors, conversely, switch on and provide a ground connection when activated. OSE output refers to open collector outputs, offering flexibility in signal management by allowing external voltage sourcing. Each type serves distinct needs in automation, safety, and monitoring systems across various industries.</p></p>
