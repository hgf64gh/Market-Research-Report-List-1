<p><ul><li>Optically Clear Adhesive</li><li>Liquid Optical Clear Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Display Bonding Adhesive Market primarily consists of two types: Optically Clear Adhesive (OCA) and Liquid Optical Clear Adhesive (LOCA). OCA is a film-based adhesive that provides excellent optical clarity and is commonly used in displays for smartphones and tablets. LOCA, on the other hand, is a liquid adhesive that cures under UV light, allowing for precise application and bonding on uneven surfaces. Both types enhance display performance by improving adhesion and visibility while minimizing reflections and distortions.</p></p>
