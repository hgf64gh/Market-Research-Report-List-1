<p><strong>The Video Surveillance Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-end ISP Chip</li><li>Front-end IPC SoC Chip</li><li>Back-end DVR SoC Chip</li><li>Back-end NVR SoC Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video surveillance chip market features various types tailored for different functions. Front-end ISP chips focus on image processing and enhancement in surveillance cameras, ensuring high-quality video capture. Front-end IPC SoC chips integrate processing capabilities within IP cameras for real-time data transmission. Back-end DVR SoC chips are designed for digital video recorders, managing storage and playback of recorded footage, while back-end NVR SoC chips handle network video recorders, enabling centralized management of multiple camera feeds for enhanced security systems.</p></p>
