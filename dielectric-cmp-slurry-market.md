<p><ul><li>Colloidal Silica Slurries</li><li>Ceria Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dielectric CMP slurry market is primarily characterized by two types: colloidal silica slurries and ceria slurries. Colloidal silica slurries are widely used for pad conditioning and delivering superior planarization on semiconductor surfaces, offering excellent surface finishes. Ceria slurries, on the other hand, contain cerium oxide particles and are favored for their exceptional removal rates and efficiency in polishing advanced materials. Both types play vital roles in enhancing the performance and reliability of microelectronic devices in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
