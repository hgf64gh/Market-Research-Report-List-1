<p><p>The standard spark plug market is primarily divided into hot and cold spark plugs, differentiated by their heat ranges. Hot spark plugs maintain higher temperatures to prevent fouling and are suitable for low-performance engines and daily driving conditions. Conversely, cold spark plugs dissipate heat quickly, making them ideal for high-performance engines where extreme temperatures are common. The choice between the two types is crucial for optimizing engine performance, efficiency, and longevity, catering to various automotive needs and driving styles.</p></p>
