<p><p>The Metered Dose Inhalers market includes several types. Dry Powdered Inhalers (DPIs) deliver medication in powdered form, requiring patient inhalation to activate the dose. Pressurized Metered Dose Inhalers (pMDIs) utilize propellants to aerosolize the medication for inhalation. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist for easier breathing, particularly for patients unable to use other inhalers. Connected Metered Dose Inhalers incorporate digital technology to track usage, enhancing adherence and management of respiratory conditions through real-time data sharing.</p></p>
