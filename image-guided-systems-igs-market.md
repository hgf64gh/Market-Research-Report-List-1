<p><p>The Image Guided Systems (IGS) market comprises various imaging technologies facilitating precision in medical procedures. Computed Tomography (CT) Scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images, while Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers high-resolution images of soft tissues. Endoscopes allow direct visualization within the body, and X-Ray Fluoroscopy provides real-time imaging for dynamic assessment. Ultrasound Systems utilize sound waves for imaging, while Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) enable functional imaging, aiding in the detection of diseases and monitoring treatment efficacy.</p></p>
