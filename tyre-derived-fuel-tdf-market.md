<p><p>Tyre Derived Fuel (TDF) is an alternative fuel produced from used tyres, leveraging their high energy content. The market is segmented into two main types: shredded tyre and whole tyre. Shredded tyre is processed into small pieces, allowing for easier handling and efficient combustion in energy recovery applications, such as cement kilns. In contrast, whole tyres are often used in specific applications where their larger size can be advantageous, providing a longer burn time and higher energy output. Both types contribute to sustainable waste management and energy production.</p></p>
