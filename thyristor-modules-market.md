Medium voltage modules, ranging from 1,000 to 35,000 volts, serve power distribution and control in various sectors. High voltage modules exceed 35,000 volts, primarily used in transmission systems and heavy industrial applications, enabling efficient power management and control in demanding environments.</p></p>
