<p><p>The stainless steel fiber market is categorized into three main types: flat, hooked, and undulated stainless steel fibers. Flat stainless steel fibers are known for their high tensile strength and are commonly used in applications requiring improved durability. Hooked stainless steel fibers enhance bond strength and are often used in concrete reinforcement. Undulated stainless steel fibers provide excellent flexibility and distribution within composites, making them ideal for applications needing enhanced toughness. Each type serves specific industrial needs, contributing to various construction and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
