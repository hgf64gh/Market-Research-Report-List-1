<p><p>The Electrical to Optical Converters market is primarily segmented into Multi-Mode and Single Mode types, catering to different communication needs. Multi-Mode converters are designed for short-distance data transmission, utilizing multiple light paths, making them cost-effective for local area networks. In contrast, Single Mode converters are optimized for long-distance communication, using a single light path to minimize signal loss and increase bandwidth, ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance and performance requirements.</p></p>
