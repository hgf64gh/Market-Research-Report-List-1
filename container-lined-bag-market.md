<p><strong>The Container Lined Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Layer Flexitank</li><li>Double Layer Flexitank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The container lined bag market primarily consists of two types: single-layer flexitanks and double-layer flexitanks. Single-layer flexitanks are designed for transporting liquids and are made from a single, robust layer, suitable for various non-hazardous liquids. In contrast, double-layer flexitanks feature an additional layer for enhanced durability and protection, making them ideal for more sensitive or hazardous materials. Both types cater to different shipping needs, optimizing safety and efficiency in bulk liquid transportation within standard shipping containers.</p></p>
