<p><ul><li>Rail Side Installation</li><li>On-rail Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway axle counting systems are crucial for monitoring train movements and ensuring safety. The market includes two main types: rail side installation and on-rail installation. Rail side installation involves placing sensors alongside the tracks to detect axle passage, offering a non-invasive solution that minimizes disruption. In contrast, on-rail installation incorporates sensors directly onto the rail cars, enabling real-time data collection and enhanced accuracy. Both systems facilitate efficient traffic management and help improve railway operational efficiency.</p></p>
