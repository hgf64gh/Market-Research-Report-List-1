<p><strong>The Traditional Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards</li><li>Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traditional probe card market is primarily classified into two types: Blade/Tungsten probe cards and Epoxy/Cantilever probe cards. Blade/Tungsten probe cards utilize sharp tungsten needles for precise contact with semiconductor wafers, providing high reliability and durability, suitable for high-density applications. Epoxy/Cantilever probe cards use flexible epoxy-based materials to create beams that deflect during testing, allowing for better alignment and softer contact, which is ideal for fragile or low-profile devices. Each type serves specific testing needs in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
