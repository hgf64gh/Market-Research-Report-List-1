<p><ul><li>Early Inica Rice</li><li>Mid/Late Indica Rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indica rice, particularly long-grain varieties, is categorized into Early Indica Rice and Mid/Late Indica Rice based on harvest timing. Early Indica Rice is harvested sooner, typically in spring or early summer, and is known for its shorter growth cycle, softer texture, and higher moisture content. Mid/Late Indica Rice, harvested later in the season, has a longer growth period, resulting in firmer grains, better storage quality, and higher yields. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences and market demands, with Early Indica often consumed fresh and Mid/Late Indica favored for longer shelf life.</p></p>
