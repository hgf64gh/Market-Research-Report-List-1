<p><strong>The PP Woven Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated PP Woven Bags</li><li>Non-Laminated PP Woven Bags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP woven bags market is categorized into two primary types: laminated and non-laminated bags. Laminated PP woven bags are constructed with a thin layer of plastic film, providing moisture resistance and enhanced durability, making them ideal for packaging bulk materials like fertilizers and grains. In contrast, non-laminated PP woven bags, made from woven polypropylene fibers without an external layer, are more economical and typically used for less sensitive products, such as sand or cement, where protection from moisture is less critical.</p></p>
