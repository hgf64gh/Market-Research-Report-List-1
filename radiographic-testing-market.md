<p><strong>The Radiographic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Real Time Radiography (RTR)</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Digital Radiography (DR)</li><li>Computed Radiography (CR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiographic Testing encompasses various market types used for non-destructive testing. Film Radiography utilizes traditional film to capture images, while Real Time Radiography (RTR) allows immediate image visualization, enhancing inspection efficiency. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed 3D images for complex structures. Digital Radiography (DR) employs digital detectors for faster image acquisition and processing, improving workflow. Computed Radiography (CR) combines conventional film techniques with digital image processing, offering a bridge between traditional and fully digital imaging systems in inspections.</p></p>
