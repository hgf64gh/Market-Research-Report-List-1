<p><p>The polyester tire fabric market predominantly includes two types: all-steel tire fabric and bias tire fabric. All-steel tire fabric features steel cord reinforcement, providing enhanced strength, durability, and resistance to punctures, making it ideal for high-performance and heavy-duty tires. Bias tire fabric, on the other hand, consists of nylon or polyester layers arranged at an angle, offering flexibility and a smoother ride, typically used in passenger vehicles. Both types cater to distinct automotive needs and preferences, driving market demand.</p></p>
