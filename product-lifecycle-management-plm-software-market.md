<p><p>Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software encompasses various market types that support different aspects of product development. PDM (Product Data Management) focuses on managing and organizing product data and documents. CAD (Computer-Aided Design) is used for creating detailed product designs. CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering) aids in analysis and validation of designs through simulations. FEA (Finite Element Analysis) is a specialized type of CAE for structural analysis. MOM (Manufacturing Operations Management) deals with optimizing manufacturing processes. Additional tools and solutions fall under "Others," catering to specific needs within PLM.</p></p>
