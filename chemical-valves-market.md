<p><p>The chemical valves market comprises several types, each serving specific functions. Gate valves provide a tight seal and are ideal for on/off applications. Control valves regulate flow and pressure, allowing for precise adjustments. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and minimal flow resistance, while butterfly valves are lightweight and suitable for large volumes. Other types include check valves and pressure relief valves, each designed for unique roles in fluid handling and process control within the chemical industry.</p></p>
