<p><p>The green ammonia gas market is primarily segmented into three electrolysis technologies: alkaline water electrolysis, proton exchange membrane (PEM), and solid oxide electrolysis. Alkaline water electrolysis uses a liquid electrolyte to produce hydrogen, offering reliability and cost-effectiveness. PEM technology employs a solid polymer membrane for higher efficiency and rapid response, making it suitable for dynamic operations. Solid oxide electrolysis, operating at elevated temperatures, enhances energy conversion efficiency and enables co-production with other chemicals, catering to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
