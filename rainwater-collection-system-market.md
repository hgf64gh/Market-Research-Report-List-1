<p><p>The rainwater collection system market is primarily divided into two types: land-based collection and roof-based collection. Land-based collection systems, such as water catchment basins or ponds, gather runoff from landscapes and are ideal for agricultural or large-scale applications. Roof-based collection systems utilize rooftops to capture rainwater, directing it into storage tanks for residential or commercial use. Both systems provide sustainable water sources, reduce dependency on municipal supplies, and promote effective water management practices in various environments.</p></p>
