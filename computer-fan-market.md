<p><p>The computer fan market is primarily divided into AC and DC fans, each serving distinct functions. AC fans operate on alternating current, typically used in larger systems like servers due to their efficiency over long distances. In contrast, DC fans use direct current, favored for their compact size, adjustable speed, and lower power consumption, making them ideal for personal computers and laptops. Both types are essential for thermal management, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of electronic devices.</p></p>
