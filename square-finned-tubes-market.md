<p><p>The square finned tubes market consists of two primary types: single metal finned tubes and bimetal composite finned tubes. Single metal finned tubes are constructed from a single type of metal, offering dependable thermal conductivity and resistance to corrosion. In contrast, bimetal composite finned tubes combine two different metals, providing enhanced performance by leveraging the strengths of each material. This allows for improved efficiency, durability, and adaptability in various applications such as heat exchangers and cooling systems.</p></p>
