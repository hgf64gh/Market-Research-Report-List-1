<p><p>The inkjet mobile printers market is divided into two main types: wire and wireless. Wired inkjet printers connect directly to a device via USB or other cables, offering stable connections but limiting mobility. In contrast, wireless inkjet printers utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology, allowing users to print from smartphones, tablets, or laptops without being tethered to the device. Wireless printers enhance flexibility and convenience, making them increasingly popular for on-the-go printing needs in diverse settings.</p></p>
