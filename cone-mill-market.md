<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cone mills are classified into vertical and horizontal market types based on their orientation and application. Vertical cone mills are designed to facilitate the downward flow of materials, making them ideal for products that require gravity-assisted processing. In contrast, horizontal cone mills are optimized for efficient mixing and granulation, offering better control over the particle size distribution. Both types serve distinct industries, catering to specific needs within pharmaceuticals, food processing, and material engineering, ensuring versatility in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
