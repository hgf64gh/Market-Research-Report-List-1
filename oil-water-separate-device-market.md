<p><p>The Oil Water Separator (OWS) market encompasses three main types: Above Ground OWS, Below Ground OWS, and Marine OWS. Above Ground OWS systems are installed on the surface, suitable for industrial and commercial applications. Below Ground OWS systems are buried and ideal for larger facilities, offering space-saving advantages. Marine OWS systems are specifically designed for ships and offshore platforms to manage oily water discharges, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and protecting marine ecosystems. Each type serves distinct operational and regulatory needs.</p></p>
