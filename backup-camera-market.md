<p><strong>The Backup Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backup camera market primarily features two types of image sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors offer superior image quality with better low-light performance, making them suitable for high-end vehicles. In contrast, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective, energy-efficient, and allow for faster processing, leading to increased adoption in mainstream cars. Both types serve the essential function of enhancing driver safety by providing a clear view of the area behind the vehicle.</p></p>
