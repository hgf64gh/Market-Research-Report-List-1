<p><p>The eye examination equipment market comprises several essential tools used in vision assessment. The phoropter is a device that measures refractive errors and determining the appropriate lens prescription. An autorefractor automates this process, quickly assessing a patient's vision by calculating refractive errors via light reflection. A lensmeter measures the prescription of eyeglass lenses, ensuring accuracy for corrective eyewear. Other equipment includes tonometers for eye pressure measurement and visual field analyzers, broadening the scope of comprehensive eye care diagnostics.</p></p>
