<p><p>The patch buzzer market is categorized into two main types: active and passive patch buzzers. Active patch buzzers generate sound when an electric current is applied, using a built-in oscillating circuit, making them more efficient and louder. In contrast, passive patch buzzers require an external sound source to operate, producing sound based on the frequency supplied. Both types serve various applications in electronics, alarm systems, and consumer devices, catering to different user needs in terms of sound output and design flexibility.</p></p>
