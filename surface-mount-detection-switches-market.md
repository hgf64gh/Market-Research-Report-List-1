<p><p>Surface Mount Detection Switches (SMDS) are categorized mainly into Miniature and Subminiature types. Miniature types are designed for applications requiring small, space-efficient switches, providing reliable performance in compact devices. They typically feature a higher actuation force and durability. Conversely, Subminiature types are even smaller, ideal for ultra-compact settings, offering lower actuation forces and sensitivity, making them suitable for precise applications. Both types cater to various industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, ensuring versatility in design and functionality.</p></p>
