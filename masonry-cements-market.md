<p><p>Masonry cements are categorized into two main types based on their consistency: standard consistency and extra thick consistency. Standard consistency masonry cements possess a balanced mix, making them ideal for general construction purposes, providing optimal workability and adhesion. In contrast, extra thick consistency masonry cements are designed for applications requiring a thicker mixture, offering enhanced stability and durability in demanding environments. These variations cater to different project needs, ensuring optimal performance in masonry construction.</p></p>
