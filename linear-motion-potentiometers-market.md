<p><p>Linear motion potentiometers are sensors that measure displacement or position. The market includes various types, such as wirewound, carbon film, and metal film. Wirewound potentiometers use a resistive wire to provide precise measurements and are ideal for high-accuracy applications. Carbon film potentiometers utilize a carbon-based layer for lower-cost solutions with moderate accuracy. Metal film potentiometers offer high stability and low noise levels, making them suitable for demanding applications. Each type caters to different needs in industrial and consumer markets.</p></p>
