<p><strong>The Display Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TFT Type</li><li>TN Type</li><li>STN Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The display polarizer market consists of various types, including TFT, TN, and STN polarizers. TFT (Thin Film Transistor) type polarizers are used in high-resolution displays, providing superior color and contrast. TN (Twisted Nematic) type polarizers are commonly found in budget displays, offering faster response times but limited viewing angles and color reproduction. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) type polarizers enhance color depth and viewing angles compared to TN, making them suitable for portable devices with moderate performance needs.</p></p>
