<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebuliser devices market comprises three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers, also known as jet nebulisers, utilize compressed air to create a mist from liquid medication, making them widely used in clinical settings. Ultrasonic nebulisers use high-frequency vibrations to produce aerosolized droplets, offering silent operation and faster medication delivery. Mesh nebulisers employ a vibrating mesh to create fine particles, providing portable options with efficient drug delivery and minimal waste, catering to varying patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
