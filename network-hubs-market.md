In an active market, hubs manage and direct traffic, like switches that amplify signals, actively processing data and ensuring efficient information flow. In contrast, a passive market relies on simple data distribution without processing, akin to basic splitters that transmit signals without alteration. Each market type plays a unique role, with active hubs enhancing performance and passive hubs serving straightforward connectivity needs within the network infrastructure.</p></p>
