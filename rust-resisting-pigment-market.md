<p><strong>The Rust Resisting Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Antirust Pigment</li><li>Chemical Antirust Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rust Resisting Pigment Market is categorized into two main types: physical antirust pigments and chemical antirust pigments. Physical antirust pigments function by forming a barrier, preventing moisture and oxygen from reaching the metal surface. They often include materials like zinc and titanium. In contrast, chemical antirust pigments react with corrosive agents to inhibit rust formation, utilizing compounds such as chromium and molybdenum. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the durability and lifespan of coated metal products.</p></p>
