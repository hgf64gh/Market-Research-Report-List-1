<p><p>The Automotive Battery Protection IC market encompasses various types, including Secondary Protection and Overcharge Protection. Secondary Protection ICs safeguard batteries from faults like short circuits and overcurrent conditions, ensuring longevity and safety during operation. Overcharge Protection ICs specifically prevent batteries from being charged beyond their safe voltage limits, which can lead to overheating, swelling, or potential failure. Together, these protection solutions are essential for enhancing battery safety and efficiency in electric vehicles and hybrid systems.</p></p>
