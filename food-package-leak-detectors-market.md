<p><p>The food package leak detection market comprises several types of technologies. Underwater bubble detectors identify leaks by observing air bubbles in submerged packages. Pressure decay detectors measure pressure changes in sealed packages to detect leaks, while vacuum decay detectors assess changes in vacuum levels to find breaches. Additionally, other methods may include tracer gas systems or fluorescent dye tests. Each type provides a reliable means to ensure food safety and extend shelf life by identifying package integrity issues.</p></p>
