<p><p>The color sensors market is primarily divided into two types: monochrome sensors and RGB color sensors. Monochrome sensors detect varying shades of a single color, making them ideal for applications requiring precise intensity measurements. In contrast, RGB color sensors can identify and differentiate between multiple colors by analyzing the red, green, and blue components of light. This capability allows RGB sensors to be used in applications like color matching, quality control, and robotic vision, enhancing automation and precision across industries.</p></p>
