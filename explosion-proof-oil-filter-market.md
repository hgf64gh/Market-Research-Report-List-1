<p><ul><li>Vacuum Type</li><li>Centrifugal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion-proof oil filter market is categorized into vacuum and centrifugal types. Vacuum-type oil filters utilize suction to draw contaminants out of the oil, ensuring a cleaner output, suitable for environments with explosive risks. Centrifugal-type filters, on the other hand, use high-speed rotation to separate impurities based on density, providing efficient filtration in demanding conditions. Both types are designed to enhance safety and performance in industries like petrochemical, mining, and manufacturing, where explosive hazards are prevalent.</p></p>
