<p><ul><li>Frog Type Dynamic Compactor</li><li>Vibration Type Dynamic Compactor</li><li>Tamping Type Dynamic Compactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic compactor market consists of three main types: Frog Type, Vibration Type, and Tamping Type. Frog Type dynamic compactors feature a heavy, hinged impact plate that delivers concentrated impact energy to the ground, ideal for deep soil compaction. Vibration Type compactors utilize vibrating plates or rollers to enhance soil density through oscillation, effective for surface layers. Tamping Type compactors employ a series of impacts and are suitable for compacting granular materials, ensuring stability and reducing settlement in construction projects.</p></p>
