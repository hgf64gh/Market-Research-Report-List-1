<p><p>The ECG Equipment and Management System market includes several types of devices designed for cardiac monitoring. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours for detecting arrhythmias. Resting ECG systems capture heart activity while patients are at rest, aiding in diagnostics. Stress ECG monitors assess heart performance under physical stress, essential for evaluating heart conditions. Event monitoring systems allow patients to record ECG events manually, facilitating remote monitoring and timely intervention in potential cardiac issues.</p></p>
