<p><p>The LFA-based cardiac marker market includes several key types: Troponin, CK-MB, Myoglobin, and Others. Troponin is the most specific marker for myocardial damage, while CK-MB is used for diagnosing heart attacks. Myoglobin is a rapid indicator of muscle injury but is less specific. Other markers may include BNP and other proteins related to cardiac stress. These tests facilitate quick diagnosis of cardiac events, enabling timely treatment decisions in clinical settings.</p></p>
