<p><p>The floating parquet floor market encompasses two primary types: engineered and solid wood. Engineered parquet flooring consists of multiple layers, with a hardwood veneer atop a plywood or HDF core, providing stability and resistance to changes in humidity. This makes it suitable for various environments. Solid wood parquet, made from a single piece of hardwood, offers authenticity and durability but is more vulnerable to moisture changes. Both types enhance aesthetics and functionality in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
