<p><p>The contact lenses for astigmatism market includes two primary types: conventional hydrogel lenses and highly breathable silicone hydrogel lenses. Conventional hydrogel lenses are made from water-containing polymer materials that offer comfort and ease of use but may have limited oxygen permeability. In contrast, highly breathable silicone hydrogel lenses are designed to allow significantly more oxygen to reach the cornea, promoting eye health and providing prolonged wear options. Both types cater to the specific vision needs of individuals with astigmatism.</p></p>
