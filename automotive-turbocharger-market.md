<p><strong>The Automotive Turbocharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Turbocharging</li><li>Exhaust Gas Turbocharging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive turbocharger market is primarily classified into two types: mechanical turbocharging and exhaust gas turbocharging. Mechanical turbocharging uses a belt-driven compressor to increase the engine's air intake pressure, enhancing performance, particularly at lower RPMs. In contrast, exhaust gas turbocharging harnesses the engine's exhaust flow to drive a turbine, compressing air for improved combustion efficiency at higher RPMs. Both methods aim to boost engine power and fuel efficiency, catering to different performance needs in the automotive sector.</p></p>
