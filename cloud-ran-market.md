<p><p>The Cloud-RAN market encompasses various components essential for efficient network performance. Baseband Units (BBUs) handle data processing, managing signal processing tasks. Remote Radio Units (RRUs) are located closer to antennas, enhancing coverage and signal quality. Optical Transport Networks facilitate high-speed data transmission over fiber optics. Servers and processors provide the necessary computational power for data handling and processing. Measurement devices ensure optimal network performance by evaluating signal quality and network health, contributing to the overall efficiency of Cloud-RAN systems.</p></p>
