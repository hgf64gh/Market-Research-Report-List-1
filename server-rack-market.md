<p><p>The server rack market comprises two primary types: wall-mounted and detached racks. Wall-mounted racks are designed for space-saving solutions, suitable for smaller installations where floor space is limited, ideal for offices or small server rooms. Detached racks, on the other hand, offer greater capacity and flexibility, accommodating larger server installations and data centers. They are typically free-standing and can support diverse hardware configurations, providing enhanced airflow and organization for more complex IT environments. Both types cater to varying operational needs and physical constraints.</p></p>
