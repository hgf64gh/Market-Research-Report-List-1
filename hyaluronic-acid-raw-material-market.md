<p><p>The Hyaluronic Acid Raw Material Market is primarily categorized into two types: microbial fermentation and animal tissue. Microbial fermentation involves producing hyaluronic acid through the fermentation of specific bacteria, offering a sustainable and vegan-friendly option. This method is often preferred for its consistency and high purity levels. In contrast, animal tissue-derived hyaluronic acid is extracted from sources like rooster combs or other animal tissues, providing a traditional method but raising concerns regarding ethical sourcing and potential allergens.</p></p>
