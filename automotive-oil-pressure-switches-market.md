<p><ul><li>Normally Open Type (NO)</li><li>Normally Closed Type (NC)</li><li>Normally Open and Closed Types (NOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive oil pressure switches are crucial components that monitor oil pressure levels in engines. The Normally Open (NO) type remains unactivated until engine oil pressure rises, signaling a safe operating condition. Conversely, the Normally Closed (NC) type activates when oil pressure drops, indicating potential issues. The Normally Open and Closed (NOC) type combines both functionalities, offering versatile monitoring solutions. These variations cater to diverse engine designs and safety requirements, driving their demand in the automotive oil pressure switch market.</p></p>
