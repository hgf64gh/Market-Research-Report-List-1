<p><strong>The Fire Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fire Alarm Systems</li><li>Addressable Fire Alarm Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire alarm system market is primarily categorized into conventional and addressable systems. Conventional fire alarm systems use a wired configuration to connect multiple detectors to a control panel, activating alarms based on zone detection. They are simpler and cost-effective for smaller areas. In contrast, addressable fire alarm systems utilize advanced technology, allowing each detector to communicate its status directly to the panel. This provides precise location identification and faster response, making them ideal for larger or more complex environments.</p></p>
