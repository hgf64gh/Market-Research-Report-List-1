<p><strong>The Chemical Metering Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Chemical Metering Pump</li><li>Hydraulic Chemical Metering Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical metering pump market is primarily divided into two types: piston chemical metering pumps and hydraulic chemical metering pumps. Piston chemical metering pumps utilize a reciprocating piston to create precise fluid displacement, offering high accuracy and control, making them ideal for applications requiring exact dosing. In contrast, hydraulic chemical metering pumps use pressurized liquid to move the diaphragm, providing high flow rates and the ability to handle viscous fluids, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes.</p></p>
