<p><p>The Human Injection Flu Vaccine market is primarily divided into two types: Trivalent and Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccines. Trivalent vaccines are designed to protect against three strains of the influenza virus: two A strains and one B strain. In contrast, Quadrivalent vaccines cover an additional B strain, offering broader protection against four influenza virus strains. This differentiation enables healthcare providers to tailor immunization strategies based on population needs, flu season severity, and circulating virus strains, enhancing overall public health outcomes.</p></p>
