<p><ul><li>EPDM-based Type</li><li>SBR-based Type</li><li>NBR-based Type</li><li>Natural Rubber-based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rubber Masterbatch market encompasses various types based on the rubber source. EPDM-based masterbatch offers excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. SBR-based masterbatch is cost-effective and widely used in automotive and industrial products. NBR-based masterbatch provides superior oil resistance, suitable for seals and gaskets in automotive and machinery sectors. Natural rubber-based masterbatch is valued for its elasticity and resilience, commonly utilized in tires and rubber goods. Each type serves specific performance needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
