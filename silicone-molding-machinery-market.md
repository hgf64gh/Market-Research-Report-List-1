<p><p>The silicone molding machinery market is primarily divided into horizontal and vertical types, each serving specific production needs. Horizontal silicone molding machinery features a side-opening design for easy mold access, ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, vertical silicone molding machinery utilizes a top-down approach, allowing for more precise mold placement and better control over the molding process. Both types cater to diverse industries by enhancing efficiency, reducing waste, and improving the quality of silicone products.</p></p>
