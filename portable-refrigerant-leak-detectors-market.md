<p><p>The portable refrigerant leak detectors market is primarily segmented into two types: infrared and semiconductor detectors. Infrared detectors utilize advanced technology to identify gas leaks by measuring the absorption of infrared light, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, semiconductor detectors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect refrigerants, providing a cost-effective option with good performance for various applications. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient HVAC systems and environmental safety by quickly identifying and quantifying refrigerant leaks.</p></p>
