<p><p>The compaction and paving equipment market consists of various machinery types used in construction and infrastructure projects. Compaction rollers are heavy machines that compress soil, asphalt, or other materials to ensure stability. Jumping jacks or rammers are used for compacting smaller areas, ideal for trenches and tight spots. Pavers lay asphalt and concrete, providing a smooth surface. Plate compactors are portable devices for compacting granular soil and asphalt, while scrapers efficiently move and level materials across job sites.</p></p>
