<p><p>The ultrasound table market comprises three main types: electric, hydraulic, and manual ultrasound tables. Electric ultrasound tables are motorized, allowing for precise adjustments in height and position with minimal effort, enhancing the convenience for both patients and operators. Hydraulic ultrasound tables utilize a hydraulic system for height adjustments, offering stability and ease of use. Manual ultrasound tables require physical adjustments by the operator, making them more basic yet cost-effective. Each type caters to different needs in medical imaging environments.</p></p>
