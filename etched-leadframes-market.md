<p><p>The etched leadframes market encompasses various package types used in semiconductor devices. QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) and DFN (Dual Flat No-lead) are favored for their compact size and thermal performance. QFP (Quad Flat Package) offers higher pin counts for complex applications. FC (Flip Chip) enables direct connections to the substrate. SOP (Small Outline Package) and DIP (Dual In-line Package) are traditional forms for through-hole mounting. SOT (Small Outline Transistor) is popular for low-power applications. Other types include specialized designs catering to unique industrial needs.</p></p>
