<p><p>The polyetheramine market is categorized into three main types: monoamine, diamine, and triamine. Monoamines contain one amine group and are often used in applications requiring flexibility and low viscosity. Diamines, with two amine groups, are prevalent in the production of epoxy resins, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Triamines possess three amine groups and are utilized for their superior curing properties in diverse applications, including adhesives and coatings, promoting high-performance characteristics in end products.</p></p>
