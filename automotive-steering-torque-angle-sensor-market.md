<p><p>The automotive steering torque and angle sensor market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Steering Mount Sensors** are attached directly to the steering mechanism, providing precise torque and angle readings during steering. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Steering Base Mount Sensors** are located at the base of the steering column, measuring the torque and angle in a more stationary position, offering reliability. </p><p>3. **Steering Column Mount Sensors** are integrated along the steering column, capturing dynamic data on the steering input for enhanced vehicle control and safety. Each type caters to specific vehicle designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
