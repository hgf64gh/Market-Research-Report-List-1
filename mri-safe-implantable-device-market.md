<p><p>The MRI Safe Implantable Device market is segmented by magnetic field strength. High Field MRI devices operate at 1.5 to 3 Tesla, widely used for general diagnostics. Low-to-Mid Field MRI, typically below 1.5 Tesla, is suitable for specific applications like orthopedics. Very-High-Field MRI, ranging from 3 to 7 Tesla, offers advanced imaging but poses higher risks for non-safe implants. Ultra-High-Field MRI, exceeding 7 Tesla, provides exceptional detail but necessitates stringent safety measures for implants due to increased magnetic forces.</p></p>
