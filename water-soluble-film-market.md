<p><p>The water-soluble film market is categorized into two main types: cold water-soluble film and hot water-soluble film. Cold water-soluble films dissolve in cold water, making them ideal for applications in packaging and laundry detergents, where temperature control is crucial. Hot water-soluble films, on the other hand, dissolve in hot water and are commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and agriculture for water-soluble pouches and packaging. Each type serves specific functions based on temperature requirements and application needs.</p></p>
