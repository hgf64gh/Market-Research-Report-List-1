<p><p>The computer printing paper market is primarily categorized into single-layer and multi-layer paper types. Single-layer paper consists of a single sheet and is ideal for standard printing tasks, offering simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, multi-layer paper comprises multiple sheets stacked together, designed for specialized applications needing enhanced ink absorption or durability. This type is often used for high-quality prints, such as photographs or marketing materials, where richer color output and detail are essential for visual appeal.</p></p>
