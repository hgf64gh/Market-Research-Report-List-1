<p><p>Diaphragm mechanical seals are crucial components in various industrial applications, designed to prevent leaks in pumps and compressors. The market is segmented into two main types: pusher type seals and non-pusher seals. Pusher type seals feature a flexible diaphragm that adapts to wear, maintaining a tight seal, while non-pusher seals rely on fixed components and do not adjust during operation. Both types serve to enhance equipment reliability and efficiency, reducing maintenance needs and operational costs in demanding environments.</p></p>
