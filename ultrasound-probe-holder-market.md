<p><p>The ultrasound probe holder market is categorized into two main types: straight probes and angle probes. Angle probes, on the other hand, feature a curved or angled design that allows for enhanced access to difficult-to-reach anatomical areas, particularly in specialized examinations. Both types are essential in medical imaging, providing stability and precision during ultrasound procedures, thereby improving diagnostic accuracy and patient comfort.</p></p>
