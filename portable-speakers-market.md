<p><p>The portable speakers market can be categorized into active and passive speakers. Active speakers contain built-in amplifiers and are designed for easy use without additional equipment, making them popular for personal use and outings. In contrast, passive speakers require external amplifiers for sound output, offering enhanced audio quality for audiophiles. The choice between these types often depends on user preferences, intended use, and desired sound quality, with active speakers prioritizing convenience and passive speakers emphasizing performance.</p></p>
