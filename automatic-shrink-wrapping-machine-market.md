Semi-automatic shrink wrapping machines require manual intervention for product placement, offering flexibility and lower initial investment, making them suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, fully automatic shrink wrapping machines operate independently, handling the entire wrapping process, which enhances productivity and efficiency for larger-scale production lines. Both types cater to diverse industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
