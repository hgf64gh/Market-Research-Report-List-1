<p><p>The Advanced Phase Change Materials (PCM) market comprises three main types: organic, inorganic, and bio-based. Organic PCMs, derived from materials like paraffin wax, offer stable thermal properties and are non-toxic. Inorganic PCMs, often based on salts, provide higher thermal conductivity and energy storage capacity but can be corrosive. Bio-based PCMs, sourced from renewable materials, promote sustainability and have a lower environmental impact. Each type serves various applications in energy storage, building materials, and temperature regulation in diverse industries.</p></p>
