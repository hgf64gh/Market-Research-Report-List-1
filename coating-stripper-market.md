<p><strong>The Coating Stripper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>The Caustic Type</li><li>The Acidic Type</li><li>The Solvent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating stripper market is categorized into three main types: caustic, acidic, and solvent-based strippers. Caustic types utilize alkaline substances to break down tough coatings, making them effective for removing paints and varnishes. Acidic types employ strong acids to dissolve metal-based coatings and rust, suited for metal surfaces. Solvent types, on the other hand, use organic solvents to dissolve various coatings, offering versatility for different materials. Each type serves distinct applications based on the coating material and surface compatibility.</p></p>
