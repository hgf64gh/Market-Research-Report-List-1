<p><p>The cold rolled steel market primarily consists of two types: steel plates and steel coils. Steel plates are flat, thick metal sheets widely used in construction, shipbuilding, and industrial applications due to their strength and durability. Steel coils, on the other hand, are thin, rolled sheets that are easier to transport and process. They are commonly used in automotive, appliance, and furniture manufacturing. Both types offer excellent surface finish and dimensional precision, making them essential in various industries.</p></p>
