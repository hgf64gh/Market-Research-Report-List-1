<p><p>The musculoskeletal diagnostic testing market includes various imaging techniques essential for evaluating bone and soft tissue conditions. X-rays provide quick assessments of fractures and joint issues, while Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DXA) focuses on measuring bone density. Computed Tomography (CT) offers detailed cross-sectional views of complex structures, while Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) excels in soft tissue visualization. Ultrasonography uses sound waves for real-time imaging, particularly useful for assessing musculoskeletal injuries. Other market types encompass emerging technologies and specialized imaging techniques.</p></p>
