<p><p>MEMS magnetic field sensors are categorized into several types based on their operational mechanisms. Capacitive MEMS magnetic field sensors utilize variations in capacitance to detect magnetic fields, offering high sensitivity. Double torsion pendulum MEMS sensors employ a pendulum mechanism that reacts to magnetic forces, providing stability and precision. Other types may include technologies like magnetoresistive or inductive sensors, catering to diverse applications ranging from automotive to consumer electronics, thus broadening the market landscape and enhancing functionality.</p></p>
