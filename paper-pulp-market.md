<p><p>The Paper Pulp market includes various types like BSKP, BHKP, and BCTMP. BSKP (Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp) is derived from softwood trees, offering high strength and brightness, ideal for premium paper products. BHKP (Bleached Hardwood Kraft Pulp) comes from hardwood trees, providing smoothness and opacity, commonly used in printing and tissue papers. BCTMP (Bleached Chemi-Thermo Mechanical Pulp) is a cost-effective option, made through mechanical and chemical processes, suitable for packaging and board applications. Each type caters to specific paper quality and end-use requirements.</p></p>
