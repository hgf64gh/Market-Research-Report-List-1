<p><p>The humanized mouse and rat model market comprises two primary types: genetic and cell-based models. Genetic models involve the modification of rodents' genomes to express human genes or tissues, enabling the study of human diseases and therapeutic responses in a more accurate context. In contrast, cell-based models focus on implanting human cells or tissues into rodents, providing insights into human immune responses, drug metabolism, and disease progression. Both types enhance translational research and drug development, bridging the gap between preclinical and clinical studies.</p></p>
