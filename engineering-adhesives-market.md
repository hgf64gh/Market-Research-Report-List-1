<p><p>The engineering adhesives market consists of various types catering to specific applications. Reactive acrylic adhesives, available as SGA or UV adhesives, offer strong bonding and quick curing. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, making them ideal for metal bonding. Cyanoacrylate adhesives provide rapid bonding for lightweight materials. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength and durability. Silicone adhesives excel in flexibility and temperature resistance, while solvent-free reactive polyurethane adhesives combine strength and environmental safety for diverse applications.</p></p>
