Oily glass ink offers vibrant colors and excellent adhesion, making it suitable for various printing techniques, particularly on non-porous surfaces. Water glass ink, on the other hand, is environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and ideal for printing on glass products like bottles and containers. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries such as packaging, automotive, and decorative glass manufacturing.</p></p>
