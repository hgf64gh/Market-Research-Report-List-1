<p><p>The noiseless tire market is categorized primarily into radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature a construction where tread and sidewall materials are arranged at right angles, providing improved fuel efficiency, traction, and noise reduction, making them ideal for modern vehicles. Bias tires, on the other hand, have layers that crisscross, offering a sturdier design, suitable for off-road conditions. Both types aim to enhance driving comfort by minimizing road noise, appealing to consumers seeking quieter driving experiences.</p></p>
