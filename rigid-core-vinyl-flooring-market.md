<p><p>Rigid Core Vinyl Flooring encompasses three main types: WPC (Wood Plastic Composite), SPC (Stone Plastic Composite), and LVT (Luxury Vinyl Tile). WPC offers a softer feel and enhanced comfort, blending wood and plastic for durability. SPC features a denser core, providing water resistance and strong impact protection, ideal for high-traffic areas. LVT, while not a rigid core, mimics natural materials with advanced printing technology, offering versatility in design. Each type serves distinct preferences and functional needs in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
