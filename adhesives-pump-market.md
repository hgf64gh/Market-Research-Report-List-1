<p><p>The adhesives pump market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to move adhesive, ensuring accurate flow and minimal shear stress, ideal for sensitive materials. Lobe pumps feature rotating lobes that create a consistent flow, suitable for thicker adhesives and providing gentle handling. Other pump types may include gear and progressive cavity pumps, each tailored to specific adhesive viscosities and application needs, ensuring efficiency and precision in the adhesive dispensing process.</p></p>
