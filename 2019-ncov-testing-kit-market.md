<p><p>The 2019-nCoV testing kit market primarily includes two types: Fluorescent PCR and Colloidal Gold tests. Fluorescent PCR involves quantitative polymerase chain reaction technology, offering high sensitivity and specificity for detecting viral RNA, making it ideal for confirmation testing. In contrast, the Colloidal Gold method utilizes immunochromatography to deliver rapid results through visual detection of antibodies, facilitating quick screening in various settings. Both types play critical roles in the effective diagnosis and management of COVID-19.</p></p>
