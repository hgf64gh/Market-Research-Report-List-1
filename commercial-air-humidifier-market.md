<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Air Humidifier</li><li>Water Spray Air Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial air humidifier market comprises various types, primarily the vapor type and water spray type humidifiers. Vapor type air humidifiers utilize heated water to produce steam, effectively raising humidity levels in large spaces. They are efficient and often used in industrial applications. In contrast, water spray air humidifiers atomize water into fine droplets, dispersing moisture into the air without heating. This method is typically preferred for maintaining humidity in facilities like greenhouses and data centers, where precise control is necessary.</p></p>
