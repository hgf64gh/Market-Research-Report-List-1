<p><p>The velocity type compressor market primarily includes axial, centrifugal, and Francis compressors, each tailored for specific applications. Axial compressors utilize rotating blades to compress air in a streamlined flow, ideal for jet engines. Centrifugal compressors convert velocity into pressure through a radial design, commonly used in HVAC and industrial applications. Francis compressors, typically seen in hydroelectric plants, employ both axial and radial flow principles, effectively managing high flow rates with variable head conditions. Each type serves distinct operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
