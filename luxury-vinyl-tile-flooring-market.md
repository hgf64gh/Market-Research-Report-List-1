<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) flooring is categorized mainly into three types: Stone Plastic Composite (SPC), Wood Plastic Composite (WPC), and others. SPC flooring combines limestone and PVC for a rigid, durable surface, offering enhanced stability and water resistance. WPC consists of a waterproof core made from recycled wood and PVC, providing a comfortable feel underfoot and effective sound insulation. The "others" category includes varied LVT formats and technologies, catering to diverse aesthetics and functional needs in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
