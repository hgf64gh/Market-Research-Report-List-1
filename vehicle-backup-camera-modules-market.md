<p><p>The Vehicle Backup Camera Modules market includes CCD and CMOS cameras, both used for rearview imaging in vehicles. CCD cameras offer high image quality, better low-light performance, and reduced noise, making them ideal for premium applications. CMOS cameras are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, suitable for mass-market vehicles. While CCD excels in clarity and sensitivity, CMOS provides faster processing and integration with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Both types cater to safety and convenience, with CCD focusing on performance and CMOS on affordability and scalability.</p></p>
