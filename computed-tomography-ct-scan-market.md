<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scan market is segmented into three main types: X-Ray Computed Tomography (CT), Ultrasonic Computed Tomography (CT), and Gamma Rays CT. X-Ray CT uses X-rays to generate detailed cross-sectional images of the body, widely utilized in diagnostics. Ultrasonic CT employs high-frequency sound waves for imaging, offering a radiation-free option beneficial in specific applications. Gamma Rays CT utilizes gamma radiation for imaging, primarily in nuclear medicine, providing insights into metabolic activity and tissue function. Each type serves unique diagnostic needs.</p></p>
