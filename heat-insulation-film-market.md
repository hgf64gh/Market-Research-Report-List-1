<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat insulation film market is categorized into two main types: endothermic films and reflective films. Endothermic films absorb heat, helping to lower indoor temperatures by reducing solar heat gain, making them ideal for warmer climates. On the other hand, reflective films work by reflecting radiant heat away from surfaces, enhancing energy efficiency and maintaining cooler indoor environments. Both types cater to various applications, including residential, commercial, and automotive, contributing to energy conservation and comfort.</p></p>
