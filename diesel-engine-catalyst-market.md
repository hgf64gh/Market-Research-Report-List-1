<p><p>The diesel engine catalyst market comprises several types, each serving specific functions in emissions control. Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) reduces carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons. Active Silencer Catalyst (ASC) minimizes noise while ensuring emissions compliance. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) uses a reductant to convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Particulate Oxidation Catalyst (POC) focuses on reducing particulate matter emissions. Additionally, "Others" may include innovative or specialized catalysts catering to emerging regulations and technologies. Together, these catalysts help meet environmental standards.</p></p>
