Semi-refined wax retains some impurities, suitable for applications like coatings and industrial uses. Other types include food-grade wax, used for coating fruits and vegetables, and ceresine wax, a blend of paraffin and microcrystalline wax, often utilized in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. These distinctions highlight tailored applications across various industries.</p></p>
