<p><p>The Portable Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) market comprises two primary types: Semi-Automated External Defibrillators and Fully Automated External Defibrillators. Semi-Automated AEDs guide users through voice prompts and visuals, requiring them to deliver a shock manually after analysis. In contrast, Fully Automated AEDs autonomously analyze the heart rhythm and deliver shocks without user intervention, making them more user-friendly for untrained individuals. Both types are crucial for rapid emergency response, enhancing survival chances in cardiac arrest situations.</p></p>
