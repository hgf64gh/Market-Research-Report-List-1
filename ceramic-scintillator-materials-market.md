<p><p>The ceramic scintillator materials market is divided into two primary types: multi-crystal and single crystal. Multi-crystal scintillators are composed of numerous crystallites, offering good light output and effective cost-efficiency, making them suitable for various applications. Single crystal scintillators, on the other hand, consist of a single, continuous crystal structure, providing superior performance in terms of light yield, energy resolution, and angular sensitivity, thus being preferred for high-precision applications in medical imaging, radiation detection, and high-energy physics.</p></p>
