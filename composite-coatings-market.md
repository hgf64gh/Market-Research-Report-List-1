<p><p>The **Composite Coatings Market** includes various types such as **Laser Melt Injection**, **Brazing**, **Electroless Plating**, and others. **Laser Melt Injection** involves embedding particles into a substrate using laser energy, enhancing surface properties. **Brazing** uses a filler metal to join materials, improving wear and corrosion resistance. **Electroless Plating** deposits a uniform coating without electricity, offering excellent adhesion and corrosion protection. Other methods include thermal spraying and chemical vapor deposition, each tailored for specific applications like aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing durability and performance.</p></p>
