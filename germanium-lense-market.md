<p><p>The Germanium lens market features various types tailored for specific optical applications. Plano-concave lenses have a flat surface and a inward-curved surface, used for expanding beams. Plano-convex lenses possess a flat surface and an outward-curved surface, ideal for focusing light. Concave-convex lenses combine both curvatures for versatile imaging applications. Aspheric Germanium lenses feature complex shapes that minimize optical aberrations, enhancing performance in infrared systems. Each type serves distinct roles in advanced optical systems.</p></p>
