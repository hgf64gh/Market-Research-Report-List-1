<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction Guidance</li><li>Laser Guidance</li><li>Magnetic-gyro Guide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Unmanned Automatic Guided Transport Vehicle (AGTV) market encompasses various types of guidance systems, each offering unique advantages. Electromagnetic induction guidance utilizes magnetic fields for precise navigation along predefined paths. Laser guidance employs lasers to detect obstacles and navigate efficiently by mapping surroundings in real-time. Magnetic-gyro guidance combines magnetic sensors and gyroscopic technology to enhance positioning accuracy and stability. These technologies enable AGTVs to operate autonomously in diverse environments, optimizing material handling and logistics processes in industries such as manufacturing and warehousing.</p></p>
