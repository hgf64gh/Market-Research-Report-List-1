<p><p>The home Wi-Fi router market is divided into two main types: fixed Wi-Fi routers and mobile Wi-Fi routers. Fixed Wi-Fi routers are stationary devices designed for permanent installation, offering robust internet connectivity for households and offices, typically connected to a broadband service. In contrast, mobile Wi-Fi routers are portable devices that provide internet access on-the-go, using cellular networks. They cater to users needing flexible connectivity while traveling, making them ideal for remote work or outdoor activities.</p></p>
