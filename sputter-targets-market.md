<p><p>The sputter targets market is categorized into three main types: metal targets, alloy targets, and ceramic compound targets. Metal targets, consisting of pure metals like copper and aluminum, are preferred for their high conductivity. Alloy targets combine two or more metals, offering unique properties for specific applications, such as improved corrosion resistance. Ceramic compound targets consist of non-metal materials, like oxides and nitrides, which are essential for dielectric and semiconductor applications. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
