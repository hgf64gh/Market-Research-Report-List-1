<p><p>The ophthalmic diagnostic devices market comprises several key types. Ophthalmic OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) provides detailed imaging of the retina and is essential for diagnosing various ocular diseases. Automatic optometry units streamline eye examinations through automated refraction and vision testing. Ophthalmic UBM (Ultrasound Biomicroscopy) offers high-resolution imaging of the anterior segment of the eye, useful for assessing diseases like glaucoma. Additionally, other devices include tonometers, perimeters, and fundus cameras, contributing to comprehensive eye care and diagnostics.</p></p>
