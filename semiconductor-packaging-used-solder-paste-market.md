<p><p>The semiconductor packaging solder paste market is primarily categorized into leaded and lead-free solder paste. Leaded solder paste contains lead as a key component, providing excellent thermal and electrical conductivity, making it suitable for specific high-performance applications despite regulatory restrictions. In contrast, lead-free solder paste eliminates lead, utilizing alternative materials like silver or tin, aligning with environmental regulations for heightened safety. Both types are crucial for ensuring reliable connections in semiconductor devices, with lead-free gaining traction due to sustainability concerns.</p></p>
