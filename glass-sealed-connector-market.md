<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass sealed connector market consists of two key types: matched seals and compression seals. Matched seals provide a precise and secure fit, ensuring minimal leakage and optimal performance in high-pressure applications. In contrast, compression seals utilize a compressive force to create a reliable seal, often used in environments subjected to thermal expansion. Both types cater to various industries requiring robust and efficient sealing solutions, such as aerospace, electronics, and medical devices, ensuring durability and reliability in challenging conditions.</p></p>
