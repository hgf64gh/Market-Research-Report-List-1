The PEEK market can be categorized into two main types: powder and particle. PEEK powder is used primarily in additive manufacturing and coating applications, offering versatility in processing. PEEK particles, on the other hand, are often utilized in injection molding and extrusion processes, providing uniformity and enhancing the efficiency of production. Both forms cater to various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and medical, due to their durability and resistance to chemicals and heat.</p></p>
