<p><p>The elastic rail fastener market comprises three main types: concrete pillow fasteners, buckle plate fasteners, and spring fasteners. Concrete pillow fasteners are typically used in heavy-duty applications, providing stability and support. Buckle plate fasteners offer efficient rail fastening with minimal movement, enhancing track safety. Spring fasteners, known for their elasticity, allow for adjustments under dynamic loading, making them ideal for varied environments. Together, these fasteners play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and performance of railway systems.</p></p>
