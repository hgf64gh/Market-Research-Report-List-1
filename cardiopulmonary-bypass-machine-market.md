<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass (CPB) machine market is primarily segmented into two types based on pump technology: single roller pumps and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps utilize a single roller mechanism to facilitate blood circulation during surgery, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, double roller pumps employ two rollers, providing enhanced flow stability and reduced blood trauma. Both types are crucial in cardiac surgeries, with their selection influenced by factors like procedural requirements, efficiency, and patient safety.</p></p>
