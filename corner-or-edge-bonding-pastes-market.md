<p><strong>The Corner or Edge Bonding Pastes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reworkable</li><li>Non-reworkable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corner or edge bonding pastes are categorized into reworkable and non-reworkable types. Reworkable pastes allow for adjustments and repositioning after application, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility during the manufacturing process. They can be softened again upon heating, facilitating repairs. Non-reworkable pastes, on the other hand, cure to a permanent bond that cannot be altered once set, providing enhanced mechanical strength and reliability for critical applications. Both types cater to specific needs in electronics and assembly industries.</p></p>
