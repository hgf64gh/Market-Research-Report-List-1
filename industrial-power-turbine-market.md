<p><p>The industrial power turbine market can be categorized into two main types: direct drive and indirect drive systems. Direct drive turbines directly convert mechanical energy from the turbine to the generator, offering high efficiency and compact design. In contrast, indirect drive systems use a gearbox to transfer energy from the turbine to the generator, allowing for greater flexibility in speed and torque management. Both types are utilized in various industrial applications, contributing to energy production and operational efficiency.</p></p>
