<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Process Gas Chromatographs market encompasses two prominent types: Gas-Solid Chromatography (GSC) and Gas-Liquid Chromatography (GLC). GSC employs solid adsorbents to separate gas mixtures based on molecular interactions, making it ideal for analyzing trace gases. In contrast, GLC utilizes a liquid stationary phase to separate gas components, offering superior resolution and quantification for volatile substances. Both methods are crucial for industries such as petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring, providing essential data for quality control and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
