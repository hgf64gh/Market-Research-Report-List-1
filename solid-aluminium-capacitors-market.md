<p><ul><li>Plug-in Type</li><li>Patch Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid aluminum capacitors market is primarily divided into two types: plug-in and patch types. Plug-in type capacitors are designed for through-hole mounting on printed circuit boards, offering durability and stable performance in diverse applications. In contrast, patch type capacitors are surface-mounted, providing a compact solution for high-density circuits. Both types are favored for their reliability, low leakage current, and high capacitance-to-volume ratio, making them suitable for consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
