<p><p>The industrial gearbox market features various types, each suited for specific applications. Helical gearboxes are known for high efficiency and smooth operation, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Worm reduction gearboxes provide significant torque reduction and allow for compact designs, suitable for low-speed applications. Planetary gearboxes offer high torque density and compactness, popular in robotics and automotive systems. Other types include bevel gearboxes and cycloidal gearboxes, catering to specialized needs across industries like manufacturing, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
