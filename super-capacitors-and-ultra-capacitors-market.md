<p><p>Super-capacitors, also known as ultra-capacitors, are energy storage devices that bridge the gap between traditional capacitors and batteries. They come in three main types:  </p><p>1. **Double Layer Capacitors** store energy through the electrostatic separation of charge at an interface, providing high capacitance.  </p><p>2. **Pseudo Capacitors** utilize fast surface redox reactions for energy storage, offering higher energy density.  </p><p>3. **Hybrid Capacitors** combine features of both types, achieving a balance of high energy and power density. Together, they cater to diverse applications in energy management and rapid power delivery.</p></p>
