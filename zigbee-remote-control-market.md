<p><p>The ZigBee remote control market is segmented into three main frequency types: 2.4 GHz, 900 MHz, and 868 MHz. The 2.4 GHz band is the most widely used, offering global compatibility and higher data rates, making it ideal for smart home devices. The 900 MHz band provides extended range and better penetration through obstacles, suited for industrial applications, especially in North America. Meanwhile, the 868 MHz band is primarily utilized in Europe for low-power, cost-effective applications, enhancing connectivity in remote environments.</p></p>
