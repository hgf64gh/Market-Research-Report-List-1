<p><strong>The Analog Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog mass flow controller market consists of two primary types: thermal pressure type and differential pressure type. Thermal pressure mass flow controllers measure flow by sensing the temperature difference caused by a heated element within the gas stream. In contrast, differential pressure types measure the pressure drop across a flow restriction to determine flow rate. Both types find applications across industries like semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing due to their reliability and precision in controlling mass flow rates.</p></p>
