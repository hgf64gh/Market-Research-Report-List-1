<p><p>The desalination technologies market comprises several key types. Reverse Osmosis (RO) utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate salt from water, making it highly efficient and widely adopted. Multi-stage Filtration (MSF) employs a series of filtration stages to remove impurities, ensuring clean water output. Multi-effect Distillation (MED) uses heat to evaporate seawater in multiple stages, condensing the vapor to produce fresh water. Other methods include electrodialysis and solar desalination, expanding the market with diverse solutions to address freshwater scarcity.</p></p>
