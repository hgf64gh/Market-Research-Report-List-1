<p><p>Amorphous fluoroplastics are high-performance materials known for their exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability. The market for these materials can be segmented into two main types: powdered resin and solution. Powdered resin is typically used in applications requiring high-temperature resistance and non-stick properties, often utilized in coatings and specialized manufacturing. The solution market, on the other hand, involves liquid forms of fluoroplastics, suitable for applications where precise coating and adhesion are needed, such as in electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
