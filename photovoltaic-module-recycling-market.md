<p><p>The photovoltaic module recycling market can be categorized into three main types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film modules. Monocrystalline modules, known for their high efficiency and space-saving design, are typically more valuable when recycled due to their pure silicon content. Polycrystalline modules are less efficient but are produced at a lower cost and have a simpler recycling process. Thin-film modules, made from various materials, offer flexibility and lightweight properties, but their recycling often involves different techniques due to diverse composition.</p></p>
