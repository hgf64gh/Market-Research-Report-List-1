<p><strong>The Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal detector market features three primary types: Pulse Induction (PI), Very Low Frequency (VLF), and Beat-Frequency Oscillation (BFO). PI detectors send intense pulse signals, making them ideal for detecting deep metallic objects, particularly in challenging environments. VLF detectors operate at lower frequencies, offering high sensitivity to small objects and providing discrimination between metals. BFO detectors use two oscillating circuits to detect metal, generally suitable for educational purposes and hobbyist use due to their simplicity and affordability.</p></p>
