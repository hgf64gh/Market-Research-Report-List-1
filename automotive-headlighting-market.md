<p><p>The automotive headlighting market consists of three main types: Halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lighting, the traditional option, offers moderate brightness and a warm color temperature but has lower energy efficiency. HID lighting delivers brighter light and improved visibility, making it popular for luxury vehicles; however, it requires a longer warm-up time. LED lighting is gaining traction due to its superior energy efficiency, long lifespan, and compact design, offering enhanced brightness and versatility in automotive applications.</p></p>
