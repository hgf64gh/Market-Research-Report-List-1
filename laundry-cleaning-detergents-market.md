<p><p>The laundry cleaning detergents market categorizes products into four main types: </p><p>1. **Anionic Detergents** - These negatively charged detergents are effective in removing dirt and grease, making them common in household laundry.  </p><p>2. **Cationic Detergents** - Positively charged, they are often used as fabric softeners and for their antibacterial properties.   </p><p>3. **Non-ionic Detergents** - These have no charge and excel in cleaning fabrics without damaging them, suitable for delicate items.  </p><p>4. **Zwitterionic Detergents** - Featuring both positive and negative charges, they are versatile and can function in various pH levels, enhancing stain removal and fabric care.</p></p>
