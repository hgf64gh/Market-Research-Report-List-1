<p><p>The PET strapping market features two main types: embossed and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping has a textured surface that enhances friction, providing better grip and load stability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, smooth PET strapping offers a sleek finish, allowing for easy handling and application, often used in lighter loads or less demanding environments. Both types are environmentally friendly alternatives to steel strapping, providing strength and durability while being lightweight and resistant to moisture.</p></p>
