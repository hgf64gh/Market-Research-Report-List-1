<p><strong>The Light Truck Tire(LT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Truck Tire (LT) market is primarily divided into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric that crisscross, providing a sturdy sidewall and excellent load-carrying capacity, often favored for off-road conditions. In contrast, radial tires have layers arranged at right angles to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and enhanced ride comfort, making them suitable for on-road applications. These differences cater to varying consumer needs and driving conditions.</p></p>
