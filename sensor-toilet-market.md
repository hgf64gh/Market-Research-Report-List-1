<p><p>The sensor toilet market primarily features two types: infrared induction and radar sensing. Infrared induction technology uses infrared beams to detect user presence, automatically triggering functions like flushing or lid opening. This promotes hygiene by minimizing contact. In contrast, radar sensing employs microwave radar waves for detection, allowing for more flexibility and precision in user recognition, even through varying environments. Both technologies enhance convenience, cleanliness, and energy efficiency in modern toilets, catering to evolving consumer demands for smart restroom solutions.</p></p>
