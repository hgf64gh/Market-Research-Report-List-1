<p><p>The Anti-Friction Coatings market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. They are suitable for applications where safety and sustainability are priorities. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents, providing better adhesion and durability in demanding environments. While they offer superior performance, they also pose higher environmental and health risks, necessitating careful handling.</p></p>
