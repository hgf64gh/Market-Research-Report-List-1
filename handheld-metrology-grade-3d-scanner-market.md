<p><strong>The Handheld Metrology Grade 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld metrology-grade 3D scanner market comprises primarily two types: laser and structured light scanners. Laser scanners utilize laser beams to capture precise 3D measurements, offering high accuracy and detail, making them suitable for complex geometries. Structured light scanners project patterns of light onto objects and analyze the deformation of these patterns to create 3D models. Both types serve applications in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and heritage preservation, providing efficient and portable solutions for high-quality 3D data capture.</p></p>
