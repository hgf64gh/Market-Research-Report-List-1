<p><ul><li>High Purity Natural Quartz Crystals</li><li>High Purity Silica Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fused silica glass market is primarily segmented into two types: high purity natural quartz crystals and high purity silica sand. High purity natural quartz crystals are sourced from natural deposits, offering exceptional clarity and low thermal expansion, making them ideal for high-end applications such as optics and semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, high purity silica sand is processed to remove impurities, and it is utilized in various industries, including glass manufacturing and construction, for its excellent melting properties and chemical resistance.</p></p>
