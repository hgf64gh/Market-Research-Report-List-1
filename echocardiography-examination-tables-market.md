<p><p>Echocardiography examination tables are essential in cardiac imaging, categorized primarily into electric, manual, and hydraulic types. Electric tables offer automated positioning and adjustments, enhancing efficiency and convenience for clinicians. Manual tables require physical effort to change positions, typically being more cost-effective but less convenient. Each type serves specific needs in echocardiography, catering to different clinical settings, budgets, and user preferences.</p></p>
