<p><ul><li>Singlemode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Small Form-factor Pluggable (SFP) transceiver module market is divided into two main types: Singlemode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Singlemode Fiber SFPs are designed for long-distance data transmission, typically over several kilometers, using a single light path. They offer higher bandwidth and lower signal attenuation. In contrast, Multimode Fiber SFPs are suitable for shorter distances, generally within a building or campus, utilizing multiple light paths to transmit data. Each type serves different networking needs based on distance and data capacity requirements.</p></p>
