<p><p>The **Paper Dyes Market** includes various types of dyes used for coloring paper. **Sulfur Dyes** are cost-effective and provide deep shades, ideal for kraft paper. **Direct Dyes** are water-soluble and bond directly to paper fibers, offering vibrant colors. **Basic Dyes** are cationic, providing bright hues but requiring mordants for fixation. **Acid Dyes** are anionic, used for specialty papers, offering excellent lightfastness and color brilliance. Each type caters to specific paper applications, balancing cost, durability, and aesthetic requirements in the paper industry.</p></p>
