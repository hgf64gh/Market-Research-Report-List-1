<p><p>The onboard dashboard camera market comprises two main types: single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel cameras typically feature one lens, capturing footage from a single perspective, usually focused on the road ahead. In contrast, multi-channel cameras are equipped with multiple lenses, enabling simultaneous recording from various angles, such as the front, rear, and sides of the vehicle. This enhanced coverage provides a more comprehensive view, improving safety and accountability by offering critical evidence in the event of incidents.</p></p>
