<p><p>The Optical Film market for Back Light Units (BLUs) comprises several key types: Reflective Film, which enhances light efficiency by redirecting emitted light back into the display; Diffuser Film, which evenly distributes light across the screen to improve uniformity and reduce hotspots; and Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF), designed to increase luminance and improve viewing angles by optimizing light transmission. Together, these films enhance overall display performance in devices such as televisions, monitors, and smartphones.</p></p>
