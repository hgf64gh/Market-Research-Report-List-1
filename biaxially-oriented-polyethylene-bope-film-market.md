<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene (BOPE) film market comprises two main types: monolayer film and composite film. Monolayer film is produced from a single layer of BOPE, offering excellent clarity, strength, and chemical resistance, making it ideal for packaging applications. In contrast, composite film combines multiple layers with different properties, enhancing functionality such as barrier protection and customizability. Both types are widely utilized in various industries, including food packaging, which demands durability and performance.</p></p>
