<p><p>Q-switched lasers are categorized primarily into air-cooled and water-cooled systems, each serving distinct applications. Air-cooled Q-switched lasers utilize ambient air for heat dissipation, making them more portable and easier to maintain, ideal for smaller facilities or mobile applications. In contrast, water-cooled Q-switched lasers employ water as a coolant to manage higher power outputs, providing better thermal stability and efficiency, making them suitable for industrial settings and demanding processes. Both types enhance precision in various laser applications.</p></p>
