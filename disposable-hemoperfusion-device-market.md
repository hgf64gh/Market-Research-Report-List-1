<p><strong>The Disposable Hemoperfusion Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Charcoal Pellet Diffuser</li><li>Resin Pellet Infuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable hemoperfusion device market encompasses two main types: charcoal pellet diffusers and resin pellet infusers. Charcoal pellet diffusers utilize activated carbon to adsorb toxins from the blood, effectively purifying it in cases of drug overdose or poisoning. Resin pellet infusers, on the other hand, use ion-exchange resins to target specific impurities, such as metabolic byproducts or toxins. Both devices are designed for single-use, enhancing patient safety and infection control while offering effective therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
